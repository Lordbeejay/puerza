Build your Strength

With PUERZA, achieve your fitness goals by planning your workout with us!

PUERZA gives you the tools you need to create a comprehensive strength training workout plan based on your desired exercise. With just a click of a button, you get a specially curated training program that fits your strength and fitness goals.

Strength Training

Our specialized workout calculator gives you a detailed program on Strength Training based on your one rep max weight.

1. Create an Account

2. Go to the Generate Workout Page

Select the type of workout you want

3. Enter your One Rep Max
